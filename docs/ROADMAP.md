# Roadmap

## Near Term

- Full production runner service with queue, container isolation, and language adapters
- Arena leaderboard route backed by persisted `ArenaRun` records
- Boss battle timed sessions, reward grants, and richer stage attempt review
- Rich admin CMS editors for paths, bosses, quests, and shop items
- Share-card image export
- More E2E Playwright coverage for authenticated flows

## Later

- Friends and private cohorts
- Team leaderboards
- Instructor dashboards
- Paid interview packs and company-specific paths
- Mobile-first flash challenge mode
- WebXR controller interactions
