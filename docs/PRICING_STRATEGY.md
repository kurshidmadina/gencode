# Pricing Strategy

Gencode monetizes measurable technical growth: challenge depth, AI mentorship, analytics, team visibility, and immersive training.

## Positioning

Gencode is the technical mastery platform built like a game. It competes with coding-practice, interview-prep, SQL, security-lab, and learning-platform products by combining:

- mixed real-world technical challenges,
- RPG progression,
- contextual Genie coaching,
- boss battles and Arena mode,
- public proof of progress,
- immersive/voice-first learning,
- team analytics.

## Default Tiers

- Free: acquire users and prove habit value.
- Starter: convert beginners who need consistent practice.
- Pro: main B2C revenue plan for serious learners and interview candidates.
- Elite: premium ARPU plan for advanced users and immersive/Insane access.
- Team: seat-based B2B expansion.
- Enterprise: custom contracts, private content, reports, SSO-ready architecture.

## Upgrade Pressure

- Free users hit challenge and Genie limits.
- Starter users see Hard, boss, and analytics locks.
- Pro users see Elite-only Extreme/Insane and immersive mode.
- Team prospects see cohort analytics and seat-management CTAs.

## Metrics to Track

- Free to paid conversion.
- Time to first challenge clear.
- Challenge limit hits.
- Genie limit hits.
- Locked-feature clicks.
- Pricing page CTA conversion.
- Checkout start to completion.
- Team lead submissions.
- Paid retention by challenge completions/week.

## Experiments

- 7-day Pro trial after first challenge clear.
- Student annual discount.
- Interview-prep bundle landing page.
- Team cohort pilot pricing.
- Elite annual founder plan.
